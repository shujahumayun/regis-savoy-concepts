Regis & Savoy Capital · Milestone 1

Homepage concepts & design systems.

Four homepage concepts across two design systems, plus the full design system behind each. Concepts 1 to 3 sit in the warm editorial system (Design System I): 1 and 2 explore the light and dark poles, and 3 sets the wordmark as a masthead spanning the full page width. Concept 4 opens a second system (Design System II), The Register: a Swiss architectural prospectus in navy and gold, built with ruled grids, tabular figures, and monospace notation. Pick the temperament that feels right and we build the rest of the site from it.

Homepage concepts

Concept 1 Light · Editorial

Bone paper, generous whitespace, left-aligned editorial grid. Services as a four-card set. Reads calm, clean, and modern — the "quiet luxury" bright room.

View concept 1
Concept 2 Dark · Gallery

Deep charcoal, gold hairlines, centered hero. Services as a numbered editorial list, with one light "breath" band. Reads cinematic, private, and weighty.

View concept 2
Concept 3 · Masthead Regis & Savoy

The wordmark is the hero — the Regis & Savoy logotype set edge-to-edge as a full-width masthead, over alternating bone and charcoal full-bleed bands stitched with gold hairline seams. Numbered distinctions lattice, four-figure stat strip, and the client's approved homepage copy throughout.

View concept 3
Concept 4 · New system (v2) The Register

A prospectus aesthetic. Navy and ivory bands ruled with gold hairlines, a four-figure stat strip set in tabular figures, and monospace ledger notation on the labels. Carries the client's approved homepage copy throughout and closes on a private consultation form. Reads institutional, precise, and understated.

Design systems

Design System I Warm Editorial

Minimal / luxury — quiet, understated, editorial restraint. A tight palette and disciplined whitespace, refined typography, and quiet, purposeful motion. The full color, type, logo, and component system behind Concepts 1–3.

View design system I
Design System II The Register

Minimal / Swiss-architectural — refined, structural, precision-led. Deep midnight and warm ivory, a single gold accent, ruled lines and monospace "ledger" notation. Reads like a considered prospectus. The full system behind Concept 4.

View design system II
