Rules

  • Headlines: Cormorant, title case, weight 500 — every one ends in a gold period. Negative tracking. Never all-caps.
  • Eyebrows: Inter 600, uppercase, wide tracking, ash grey, led by a gold index numeral (01–05) and a short gold hairline.
  • Body: Inter 400–500, sentence case, on a softened tier (never pure black/white on its ground). strong lifts to full ink/paper.
  • URLs: lowercase, no https://. Marcellus is logo-only — it never appears in body or headlines.

07 Spacing & Motion

Rhythm.

Motion

  • Transforms & opacity only — no animated filters, blur, noise, or marquees.
  • Reveal: sections fade + rise on scroll (IntersectionObserver, .reveal → .in), .8s ease, staggered .09/.18/.27s.
  • Hover: buttons lift 2px; links grow a gold underline; service rows slide right; portraits zoom 1.04.
  • All motion is gated behind a .js class and disabled under prefers-reduced-motion.

08 Don'ts & Constraints

The guardrails.

Brand / visual

  • Never a generic purple-to-blue "AI" gradient.
  • Never fill large areas with gold — it's a line / numeral / single-CTA accent.
  • Never more than the one gold accent at weight per composition.
  • Never rounded corners on the monogram or blocks — hairline squares only.
  • Never all-caps or exclamation headlines; always the gold period.
  • Never fake urgency, promise returns, or crowd the page with claims.

Technical (carry into the Wix build)

  • Self-contained — one HTML file per page, CSS/JS inline. No external JS libraries.
  • Google Fonts is the only external runtime resource.
  • Transforms & opacity only for animation — no marquees / animated filters.
  • Works fully without JavaScript — JS only enhances.
  • Mobile-first, smooth on low-end laptops; respect reduced-motion.
