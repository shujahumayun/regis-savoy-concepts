The Firm

Independent by Structure. Personal by Design.

Regis and Savoy Capital is an independent Multi-Family Office built on a simple belief: meaningful wealth management begins with understanding people, more than products.

Our selective approach to client relationships enables us to dedicate meaningful time, attention, and expertise to every family we serve.

Our leadership team brings together more than 90 years of collective experience across leading global financial institutions, including American Express, Barclays, Citibank, Deutsche Bank, Morgan Stanley, ICICI Prudential, and Kotak.

Investment Philosophy

A Disciplined Approach to Long-Term Wealth.

01

Capital Preservation

Protecting capital remains the foundation of every investment decision. We prioritize safeguarding wealth before pursuing growth, ensuring stability across market cycles.

02

Independent Thinking

Objective decisions guided by facts, analysis, and client interests. We hold no inventory and distribute no products; our recommendations serve only your plan.

03

Long-Term Perspective

Investment decisions guided by long-term objectives rather than short-term market movements. Our horizon is measured in generations, not quarters.

04

Selective Deployment of Capital

Investing only where opportunities are well understood and risks are carefully evaluated. We evaluate far more than we recommend, and we say no more often than yes.
